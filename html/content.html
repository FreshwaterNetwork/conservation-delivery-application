Wetlands provide many services for people and wildlife: improving water quality, reducing flood impacts, helping streams flow, supporting fish and wildlife habitat, and others.

But not all wetlands are the same. The types and amounts of services each wetland provides depends on habitat type, location in the watershed, surrounding land-use, soil characteristics, and many other factors.

The Wetlands and Watersheds Explorer takes these factors into account to help you to identify the top tier of sites that meet your needs. For further context and details see the report.
